Who We Are at Locksmith Direct

Locksmith Direct is run by Jeffrey Seymour. Locksmith Direct want to give customers a better choice regarding needing a locksmith service. Jeff has many years of experience within the industry and has run his own successful locksmith company on the south coast of the UK since 2014. It will be, for the most part, Jeff, who you speak to when you contact Locksmith Direct. We are committed to giving our customers excellent service at a price that is fair and transparent, so you know that you're getting tremendous value for your money.

Jeff is fully trained as a locksmith and continues to train in an ever increasing industry where new locks and mechanisms are coming onto the market. Picking locks is a fine art, and staying ahead of the lock manufacturers is paramount. Jeff does this by regular training with retired Locksmiths who are happy to pass on the skills they have gained over the years. It’s not just constant training, but also Jeff has a full DBS certificate and is a member of the UK Locksmith Association.

Locksmith Direct is a nationwide company, our goal is to use local to our customer's Locksmiths who are also fully trained, hold a DBS certificate and have been vetted by Jeff. Hence, you know the locksmith on their way to your emergency lockout will provide outstanding service. They will leave you with an itemised invoice, so you know there will be no hidden charges on job completion.
